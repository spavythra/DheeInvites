So — who exactly is Dhrithi?
A normal school day. Buckle up. 😄
Panda cuddles, bee buzz, and excavator energy all belong in this birthday story.
Spoiler: there's no such thing as "normal."
Bees, butterflies, panda paws, and one very determined excavator all show up here.

The forest theme grows with every detail — tiny insects, soft animals, and an excavator that still steals the show.

06:58
A voice at the window: "Moi moi, Akkuli!!" An excavator (her word, we've stopped fighting it) sits two streets away, doing nothing. She waves it in like a landing strip. It leaves. "Heippa!!" 😭 She demands it return. It doesn't.

Mission accomplished, she turns to the next task: "Wake up Daddy!" 📣 Delivered directly into his ear, at a volume that makes the excavator look subtle. 😴➡️😱
08:15 · To Pomppu
Recently promoted from Touhula 🎓. On the way, every vehicle gets a full sound effect — planes get the arms-out treatment with her own jet engine noise 💨, trains get the rumble, bikes get an oddly accurate vroom 🏍️. Whatever's passing, she becomes its soundtrack.

Also: first official school note arrived. She loved a friend so much, she bit them. 🦷💛 Working on the distinction. 😂
16:00 · Outside
Two shifts outside, one inside (our idea, not hers). 🌱 Sand and soil — she isn't playing in the garden, she is the garden.

The panda soft toy gets a sprint-and-hug like it owes rent 💛; everything else gets a wave. The whole toy zoo gets lined up for "JUMP!" 🫵 — none of them do, so she jumps for them. Then "Nykyu!!" = sleep, and they all drop at once. Toys: obedient. Humans: still learning. 😂
Bonus Level · K-Citymarket Turtola
Her favourite place on Earth. 🏪 Every single aisle gets the full inspection — she runs row to row, touching, testing, rearranging as needed.

This is also the most dangerous part of any day: she moves faster than two adults can track, and "where did she go" is asked at least once per visit. 🚨 We don't lose her. We just temporarily can't find her. 😅

For reference: Leo's Leikkimaa is the paid version of this experience. K-Citymarket is the free one. She rates them equally. 🎟️➡️🆓
17:30 · Bath & Lelukirja
The book comes everywhere 🚿📖. She points, asks "What's that?", we answer, she nods — then points at the same picture again. Not a question. A memory test. For us. 🧠 Wrong answer? Corrected. Patiently. Repeatedly. 👩‍🏫
18:45 · The Call
Paati & Thatha summoned on schedule. 📱 She plays, they watch — with their own snacks, she's not sharing. 🫙 Hang up? Called straight back to keep watching. To her, generous. 👴👵😂

Somewhere in here, a random word drops and the room goes quiet — Tamil? English? Finnish? Kannada? 🔍 The "no" set covers all four: Ei, No, Venam, Odhdhu. Whichever lands, it's final. 😤
20:30 · Bedtime
On the way, every star gets counted, one by one. 🌙 Then: lullabies — no. Rocking — no. Swing — absolutely not. None of the normal things work.

The only thing that does: Dad buckles her in 🚗, Mum drives, full loop, Dad reaches back and holds her hand. That's the whole trick. 🤝 We've driven more kilometres putting her to sleep than to any real destination. 😂
